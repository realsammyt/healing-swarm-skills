The 7 Ethics Principles

1

First, Do No Harm

Medical and psychological safety are absolute priorities.

2

Honor All Traditions

No appropriation. Proper attribution always.

3

Evidence with Humility

"May help" not "will cure." Honest about what we know.

4

Empower, Don't Control

User autonomy is sacred. Provide information, don't prescribe.

5

Privacy as Sanctuary

Healing data stays with the healer. Local-first architecture.

6

Accessible to All

Design for the most vulnerable users. No gatekeeping.

7

Continuous Improvement

Learn from outcomes. Update with evidence.
