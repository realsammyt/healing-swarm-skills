# Holotechnica Stack Example

A complete example demonstrating the Holotechnica skill lifecycle for designing an endo-technology experience stack targeting sustained equanimity.

## The Skill

| Property      | Value                                                                  |
| ------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Name**      | `equanimity-stack`                                                     |
| **Purpose**   | 30-minute standard stack targeting equanimity (altered trait)          |
| **Framework** | Holotechnica (Smith, Museum of Consciousness)                          |
| **Evidence**  | Theoretical (stacking methodology) + Moderate (individual ingredients) |

## Stack Design

A 3-ingredient stack targeting equanimity as an altered trait (not just a temporary state):

| Order | Ingredient | Duration | Evidence Level   | Purpose                 |
| ----- | ---------- | -------- | ---------------- | ----------------------- |
| 1     | Breathwork | 10 min   | Well-established | Autonomic regulation    |
| 2     | Body Scan  | 10 min   | Well-established | Interoceptive awareness |
| 3     | Silence    | 10 min   | Moderate         | Integration, non-doing  |

### Transition Design

Each transition is guided (not abrupt):

- **Breathwork → Body Scan:** "Let the breath find its natural rhythm as attention moves to the body..."
- **Body Scan → Silence:** "Release the scanning. Let awareness rest without an object..."

## Research Basis

> **Evidence level: Theoretical (stacking) + Moderate (ingredients)**
>
> Holotechnica stacking methodology is theoretical — developed by Carl Hayden
> Smith at the Museum of Consciousness. The hypothesis is that combining
> endo-technologies in deliberate sequences can cultivate altered traits
> (Goleman & Davidson 2017), not just altered states. Individual ingredients
> (breathwork, body scan) have moderate to well-established evidence bases.
> The combinatorial effects of stacking have not been independently validated.

## Additive Contraindication Logic

The stack inherits contraindications from ALL ingredients:

| Condition           | Breathwork | Body Scan | Silence | Stack Result |
| ------------------- | ---------- | --------- | ------- | ------------ |
| Respiratory issues  | CAUTION    | OK        | OK      | **CAUTION**  |
| Active dissociation | RELATIVE   | RELATIVE  | OK      | **RELATIVE** |
| Seizure disorder    | ABSOLUTE   | OK        | OK      | **ABSOLUTE** |

**Rule:** If ANY ingredient is contraindicated, the entire stack is contraindicated at that severity.

## Safety Review: Passed

- Stacking methodology labeled "Theoretical" throughout
- Individual ingredient evidence levels stated separately
- Additive contraindication logic applied
- Pharmaco-technologies HARD EXCLUDED
- Trait vs. state distinction maintained
- "May support" language (never "will produce")
- Grounding before and after stack
