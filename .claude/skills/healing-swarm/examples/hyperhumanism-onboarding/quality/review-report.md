## Evidence Honesty Review

### Checklist

- [x] Hyperhumanism labelled a theoretical framework from Carl Hayden Smith
- [x] The satellite-nav and spatial-memory association framed as correlational,
      not causal (Dahmani and Bohbot, 2020)
- [x] Awe and wonder research framed as associational with individual variation
      (Piff et al., 2015)
- [x] Endo-technology evidence levels copied from the taxonomy (method-of-loci
      Well-established as a mnemonic; sense-of-place use Preliminary)
- [x] "Research suggests" language used throughout; no "science proves"
- [x] No outcome promised; flat results named as valid
- [x] Combinations of practices treated as Theoretical as a whole

### Notes

- The technology-displacement research file is careful to say the app is not
  shown to damage anything, only that leaning on it tends to mean exercising
  one's own sense of place less. That is the honest reading.
- The evidence note in the manifest states that evidence does not compound.

## Recommendations for Future Versions

1. Offer a second worked example where the carried-forward capacity is presence
   rather than wayfinding, to show the inquiry generalising.
2. Add an optional consent-first relational practice at the ecology stage, so
   the relational cluster has a starting point.
3. Include a short longitudinal check-in that revisits the literacy baseline
   after several weeks, per `shared/outcome-measurement.md`.
