# Family Nexus Healing — For You

This is a small, private app that holds your family's healing practices in one place. It works on your phone, offline, and it belongs entirely to you.

## What This Is

This app gives you access to the practices your care team has put together for your family — breathing exercises, sensory walks, visit preparation, bedtime rituals, and more. It organizes them by who is using them (the whole family, mom alone, or dad alone) and surfaces the right ones based on the time of day.

There is a "How We Feel" section where each family member can record one word or one color each day. Nothing complicated. Just a small way to notice, together, how the days are going.

## What This Is Not

This app is not treatment. It does not replace your therapist, your case worker, or your support team. It does not diagnose anything. It does not give medical advice. The practices here come from the research and the protocols your team has reviewed, but using them is always your choice.

If something does not feel right, stop and talk to your care team.

## Your Privacy

**Your data never leaves this device.** There are no accounts, no logins, no servers, no tracking, no analytics. Everything you enter — feelings, visit dates, contact numbers — stays in your phone's local storage. Nobody can see it except you.

No one at any organization has access to what you enter here. Ever.

## How to Install

You do not need to download this from an app store.

**On iPhone or iPad:**
1. Open this page in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (the square with the arrow)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

**On Android:**
1. Open this page in Chrome
2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"
4. Tap "Add"

After you add it, the app works completely offline. You do not need an internet connection.

## How to Use It

When you open the app, you choose who you are right now:

- **Family** — for when you are together with the children. Shows time-of-day suggestions, all family practices, and the "How We Feel" log.
- **Father** — one thing at a time, in large clear text. One practice, one "Begin" button. Swipe for alternatives.
- **Mother** — your private space. Quick check-ins, fast resets, grief window scheduling, and your support contacts.

You can switch between modes at any time by tapping "Home."

## Backing Up Your Data

In Mother mode, go to "My Space" and tap "Export backup." This saves a file to your phone that contains everything — your feelings log, visit dates, and settings. Keep this file somewhere safe if you want to preserve your data.

## The Emergency Button

In Family mode, there is always a button at the bottom of the screen that says "Need help now? Tap to call 988." It calls the Suicide and Crisis Lifeline directly.

In Mother mode, the "I'm at the edge" button shows your stored support contacts (therapist, trusted person) and the 988 line. You can add those numbers in My Space so they are always one tap away.

## If Something Goes Wrong

If the app stops working, try closing it completely and reopening it. If that does not help, you can clear your browser cache for this site. Note that clearing data will remove your saved feelings and settings — consider exporting a backup first.

## One Last Thing

You are doing hard, important work. This app is just a small tool to help you find the right practice at the right time. The real work is yours, and it matters.
